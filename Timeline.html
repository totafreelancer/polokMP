Zunaid Ahmed Palak

STATE MINISTER,ICT DIVISION

1980

Zunaid Ahmed Palak was born on May 17, 1980 at Singra, Natore. Faiz Uddin Ahmed (Late) and Ms. Zamila Ahmed gave birth to this boy who later became their pride. Among his five brothers and sisters Palak was the youngest one. His father was active in politics and largely inspired by the ideology of Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman.

1994

At the age of 14 when Palak was in class VI, he formed a club name "Durdom" (indomitable), later this club turned into a center of sports and socio-cultural activities for local children. He was greatly inspired by the scouting movement of Robert Baden-Powell during his school life. He participated in 1st Asia Pacific Jamboree held in Mouchak, Gazipur in 1994 as a team leader.

1995

Palak completed his high school study from Singra Damdama Pilot School and College. He successfully passed his Secondary School Certificate (SSC) examination in 1995 with a brilliant result.

1997

After finishing his high school from Singra Damdama Pilot School in 1995, Palak got admitted in Rajshahi Government College to finish his higher secondary. In 1997 he successfully achieved his Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) under Rajshahi Board.
